Welcome young adventurer to the dungeon of Knowledge! The Thupan Dungeon!!!

Existing since the ancient times, this dungeon has charmed many adventurers into challenging it with their strength.
Full of vicious monsters, this collection of ancient ruins is deadly for any brave challenger.

Having heard the rumours about the great treasure, kept deep inside the dungeon, you have decided to conquer it.

After receiving the blessings of your villagers, you began your journey to the great dungeon...

Weeks later, you arrived in the dungeon and at last passed its doors...

In the entrance room you saw the welcoming message of the ones who came before you...

"You seem to be in haste, not sitting to think before challenging the dungeon of Knowledge...

we welcome you young adventurer...

welcome to your grave..."



THE ADVENTURE BEGINS!!
